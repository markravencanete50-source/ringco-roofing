Common questions

Claims, answered straight.

Will filing a claim raise my insurance rates?
Storm-damage claims are "acts of God" in Oklahoma — insurers can't single you out for a rate increase for filing one. Rates in hail-prone areas rise regionally whether you file or not, so leaving legitimate damage unclaimed only costs you.
What if my claim was already denied?
Denials are often about documentation, not damage. We re-inspect, photograph everything the first scope missed, and request a re-adjustment with our report in hand. Many of our approved claims started as denials.
How fast can you get on my roof after a storm?
We run 24/7 emergency response. Tarping and board-up usually same-day; full documented inspections within 24–48 hours of your call.
Do I have to use the contractor my insurer suggests?
No. Oklahoma law gives you the right to choose your own contractor. Insurer-preferred vendors work to the insurer's scope — we work to yours.
